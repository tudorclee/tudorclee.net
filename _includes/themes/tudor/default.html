We are proud to support sports in South Auckland
In 2009 we became founding sponsor of the 0800BUSTED Tongan Eagles. We won 38-12 against the favored Central Stallions to become Champions of the first Auckland Tongan League Competition.
In 2010 we were sponsors of the Mangere East Hawks Premiers Rugby League team. The team made some great come from behind victories and we are very proud of them. This was our player of the year.
This year 0800BUSTED has supported Isaac "Speedy" Paparoa. Speedy has been boxing out of 247 Youth Gym in Otara. We have travelled around Auckland and as far north as Wellsford to see his matches and have made sure all his supporters are in the famous Speedy T-Shirts.
